Here's a great Zero Click Marketing case study: Mark Powell Bespoke.

40 year old tailor in London, having his best ever year of business. Really just 4 reasons why:

1) Classic menswear and tailoring is having a resurgence (and he was well poised to execute)

2) A product that fits perfectly with what content creators in this space want (including his personality and eccentricity)

3) Serious investment in one Zero Click channel - Instagram - the perfect match for his product/audience overlap. And not just that, but he's gotten really good at it in the last 3 yrs (go look at videos from 2021/22 and you'll see the improvement in metrics, style, and messaging)

When I run SparkToro reports in this space, it's so impressive to see how thoroughly he's covered almost every source of influence classic menswear folks follow/read/watch/listen-to. So also 4) Great PR 😎. Right sources. Right pitch. Right relationships.
