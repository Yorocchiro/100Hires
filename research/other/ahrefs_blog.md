# 10 SEO Trends I’ve Seen Firsthand in 2026 (With Data)

**Source:** https://surferseo.com/blog/seo-examples/

### Summary

Ahrefs analyzed industry wide SEO shifts using their own data. 

1. **Query Fan-Out** AI search engines generate multiple hidden queries from one prompt. 
Solution: build topic clusters, not single pages. Up +2,550% YoY in search interest.

2. **AI Overviews reduce clicks by 58%** Google keeps more users on the SERP. 
The unit economics of SEO have fundamentally changed.

3. **AI Content is normalized** Infrastructure around AI models (agents, MCPs, tool 
calling) now allows genuinely good AI content. Detection interest is falling.

4. **Search Everywhere Optimization** Traffic is fragmenting across YouTube, TikTok, 
Reddit, Amazon. Visibility on these platforms now feeds AI answer visibility too.

5. **Semantic SEO & Entity Optimization** LLMs build brand understanding from 
training data context. Consistent entity representation across the web matters more.

6. **SEO Agents** Agentic AI can now automate keyword research, content updates, 
internal linking, and site audits. Searches for "SEO AI agent" up +285% YoY.

7. **llms.txt is largely useless** 97% of llms.txt files are never fetched. No major 
AI provider uses it. Not worth the time.

8. **"Best" listicles losing power** Google is penalizing self-promotional lists. 
67.6% of "best software" SERPs had authors ranking themselves #1.

9. **Information-only sites declining** Pure info blogs down ~72% from peak traffic. 
AI Overviews now answer most informational queries directly.

10. **Brand building is the new SEO** Branded web mentions correlate with AI 
visibility at 0.66–0.71. The more your brand is mentioned, the more AI cites you.
